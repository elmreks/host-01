# Methodology

Host McHostface scores each option on a 1–5 scale, where higher is better.

## Core weighting

The overall score is calculated from weighted category scores with extra emphasis on starter-friendliness:

- simplicity: 1.35
- stability: 1.2
- auth: 1.1
- payments: 1.05
- database: 1.05
- pwa: 1.0
- frontend_hosting: 0.9
- backend_functions: 0.9
- storage: 0.7
- pricing_clarity: 0.85
- lock_in_risk: 0.8
- scalability_confidence: 1.0

Lock-in risk is normalized so that a higher score still means better. In practice, that means a 5 represents lower lock-in / easier portability.

## Important note

These numbers are useful, not objective truth. Some platform qualities are measurable; others are judgement calls based on practical starter experience.
